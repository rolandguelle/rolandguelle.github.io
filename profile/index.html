Speaker Profile

Roland Guelle

Roland has been working as a software, product and technology expert since 1996. As VP Technology / CTO, he most recently successfully built up the international IT company Avenga with more than 4,000 employees.
Prior to that, he managed the software company Sevenval with 200 employees as CTO and sold it together with his partners. In addition to technical concepts and architectures, Roland specializes in building and optimizing software organization, methodologies and technologies. In the past, he has successfully implemented projects for various clients from startups to corporations, as well as developed SaaS and license products.
Roland does his own programming and speaks at conferences. Roland leads the Milecrew as managing partner.Roland supports clients as an interim CTO and leads Milecrew as managing partner.
