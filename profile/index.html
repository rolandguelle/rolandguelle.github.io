Speaker Profile

Roland Guelle

As Vice President Technology Roland Guelle is responsible for technology, research and development at Avenga. With 2.500 IT professionals around the globe Avenga is the champion for digital transformation and modern, fast and secure IT solutions.
Roland (★1976) has been with Avenga since 2019.
Prior to that, he worked as CTO of Seven­val Tech­nologies GmbH and several years as a software developer for various other companies. Roland still develops himself today, speaks at developer conferences and twitters as @rolandguelle.
